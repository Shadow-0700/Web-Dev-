AVISHKAR

Step into the vibrant world of Avishkar, MNNIT's annual techno-management extravaganza, where innovation and brilliance collide! Happening in the spirited month of March, Avishkar is the ultimate playground for students to sharpen their technical and managerial skills, sculpt outstanding projects, weave valuable networks, and sculpt a stellar CV. This dynamic festival is not just a celebration within the walls of MNNIT - it's a nationwide showcase of talent. Every department and technical club at MNNIT hosts riveting competitions, drawing participants from across the nation. These contests are the battlegrounds where aspiring technocrats engage in fierce battles, embodying the spirit of "Ideate, Innovate and Create". Apart from these competitions, many inspiring talks by eminent people in the industry and academia and other activities also happen during Avishkar, enlightening and preparing the participants for many challenges that lie ahead.
